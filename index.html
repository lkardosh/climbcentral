What is Climb Central?

Climb Central is a blog dedicated to providing key information about the many aspects of rock climbing. Rock climbing is an activity where participants climb up, down, or across artificial rock walls or natural rock formations. The goal is to reach the top or endpoint of the route. This can be done with different types of equipment depending on the route and difficulty level. It is a physically and mentally demanding sport with many different variations.
